## Build the future of diabetes tech with us

Now in its **third year**, HackDiabetes26 is a small, focused, in-person hackathon. We keep it intentionally intimate — **under 75 participants** — so the weekend stays hands-on and the conversations stay real.

The people in the room are the ones who actually move diabetes technology forward: software engineers, data folks, designers, clinicians, and the people living with diabetes and caregivers who know exactly what needs building. This is the #WeAreNotWaiting spirit in one room, and many projects that start here go on to be used by the wider Nightscout community.

Sponsors can get genuine face time with every participant, and your support is visible all weekend.

---

## Why sponsor?

- **Direct access, not noise.** With fewer than 75 builders in the room, you'll have real conversations — not a booth crush.
- **Meet hard-to-find talent.** A focused crowd of people already working in diabetes and health tech.
- **Align with a mission people believe in.** The #WeAreNotWaiting community is loyal and grateful to the partners who show up for them.
- **Fund real impact.** Sponsorship keeps the event affordable for participants and supports the Foundation's open-source work year-round.
