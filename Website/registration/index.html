What is HackDiabetes?

A weekend to build what diabetes needs.

HackDiabetes is an annual hackathon run by The Nightscout Foundation, bringing together a passionate community of developers, data scientists, clinicians, and people living with diabetes.

Over an intensive weekend, teams tackle real problems in diabetes management — from CGM integrations and closed-loop insulin delivery to data tools and patient empowerment apps. Everything built is open-source and community-owned.
